There's a large amount of VTubers

Normally, this data would be very difficult to track, due to the nature of how VTubing works for the masses. However, the folks at User Local, a company that keeps track of web analytics, were able to calculate the number of VTubers around the world. Since December 2017 to October 2021, there were able to determine that their were around 16,000 VTubers around the world.

Data provided by User Local

As you can see, this quick snap shot of data shows the ever growing popularity of this medium and it's continuous expansion.
